Daily
itinerary

7 days · 6 nights
01
Bishkek · Ala Archa
Capital City &
Mountain Gorge
Arrival in Bishkek, transfer and check-in. City tour: Ala-Too Square, Manas statue, Victory Square, Osh Bazaar. Afternoon drive to Ala Archa National Park (40 km). Trek to Ak-Sai waterfall (2,700 m) through pine forest.
City Mountains Hiking 40 km
02
Issyk-Kul · Cholpon-Ata
Pearl of
Central Asia
Drive to Issyk-Kul (250 km). En route — Burana Tower (XI century). Then — Cholpon-Ata petroglyphs: open gallery of rock carvings from 9,000 BC. Night on the north shore.
Lake History Petroglyphs 250 km
03
Jeti-Oguz · Karakol
Seven Bulls &
Broken Heart
Jeti-Oguz gorge — red rock formations 'Seven Bulls' and 'Broken Heart' amid pine forests. Drive to Karakol (45 km). Dungan Mosque in Chinese style. Dinner with a local Dungan family.
Canyon Culture Gastro 45 km
04
Skazka · Bokonbaevo · Kochkor
Fairy-Tale Canyon
& Eagle Hunting
South shore of Issyk-Kul (280 km). Skazka canyon — red-ochre formations with lake views. In Bokonbaevo — eagle hunting show. Kochkor: masterclass in traditional felt-making.
Canyon Eagle Felt art 280 km
05
Son-Kul · 3,016 m
Yurt Camp
Above the Clouds
Ascent to Son-Kul lake (140 km) — 3,016 m above sea level. Treeless nomad plateau. Horse riding, yaks, golden eagles. Kumiss-making masterclass. Night in a traditional yurt.
Lake Yurt Nomads Horses 140 km
06
Son-Kul → Bishkek
Return &
Farewell Dinner
Drive back to Bishkek (250 km), lunch on the way. Free afternoon — Osh Bazaar, rest. Evening farewell dinner at a traditional Kyrgyz restaurant.
Transfer Bishkek Free time 250 km
07
Bishkek · Airport
Breakfast &
Departure
Breakfast at the hotel. Transfer to Manas International Airport. End of tour — Best Lakes of Kyrgyzstan.

Tour Highlights

Four moments
you'll never forget

Eagle Hunting
Ancient art of eagle hunting — a living transfer of nomadic tradition. Special show with a local berkutchi in Bokonbaevo on Issyk-Kul's south shore.
Day 4 · Bokonbaevo
Yurt at 3,016 m
Night in a yurt camp on the Son-Kul high plateau. Hundreds of yurts, countless herds, starry sky with zero city lights.
Day 5 · Son-Kul
Petroglyphs 9,000 BC
Open-air rock art museum in Cholpon-Ata — wolves, ibexes, snow leopards and ritual scenes from 9,000 BC to 3,000 AD on a single hillside.
Day 2 · Cholpon-Ata
Felt-Making Workshop
Masterclass in creating traditional Kyrgyz shyrdak in Kochkor. See the whole process and create your own piece of felt carpet to take home.
